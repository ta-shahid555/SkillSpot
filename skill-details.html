Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior design experience?

No prior design experience is required. This course is designed to take you from beginner to professional level. We start with the fundamentals and gradually build up to more advanced concepts and techniques.

What software do I need for this course?

You'll need Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. You can download free trials from Adobe's website if you don't already have them. The course covers how to install and set up these programs.

How long will it take to complete the course?

The course includes about 25 hours of video content. Most students complete it in 4-6 weeks by dedicating 5-6 hours per week. However, you can go at your own pace as you'll have lifetime access to the course materials.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Yes! When you complete all course modules and submit the final project, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your resume or LinkedIn profile. Click here to see a sample certificate.

Can I get help if I'm stuck on something?

Absolutely! You can ask questions in the course discussion area, and our instructor or teaching assistants will respond within 24 hours. There's also an active student community where you can get feedback on your work.
